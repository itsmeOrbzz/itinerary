# Transportation Tips

## Singapore
- Use MRT whenever possible
- Use Grab for luggage-heavy transfers
- Buy EZ-Link or use contactless card

## Kuala Lumpur
- Grab is highly recommended
- Traffic can be heavy at night
- Keep small cash available
